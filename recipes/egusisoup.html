Egusi soup

Egusi soup is a delicious delicacy eaten by most Nigerians

Description

Egusi soup is an exotic hearty food that will satisfy your taste buds. It is a staple in most West African homes and it is an uncomplicated one-pot meal that is often accompanied by swallows like Fresh garri (Eba), Amala, Semovita, Pounded yam, Fufu, and the likes.
