Moi-moi

What do you know about Moi-moi?

Moi-moi is a traditional Nigerian meal made from beans, onions, peppers, spices, and other ingredients. It is delicious, easy to make, and inexpensive.

Ingredients

  • Peeled black-eyed peas
  • Hapanero peppers
  • Onions
  • Chili pepper
  • Bakeware
  • Crayfish
  • Fish chunks or liver
  • Salt
  • Seasoning of your choice
  • Vegetable Oil/Palm oil

Steps in preparing Moi-moi

  1. Soak the beans
  2. Peel your beans
  3. Sieve the skin of the beans out
  4. Blend the beans along with some hapanero and chili peppers
  5. Turn the batter into a bowl and add some vegetable Oil/Palm oil with salt and seasoning of your choice
  6. Add fish chunks or liver
  7. Portion the batter in some bakeware
  8. Put the pot on the stove and add boiled water.
  9. Arrange the Moi Moi in the pot and cover
  10. Allow it to steam for like 45 minutes
  11. Carefully remove the Moi Moi from the plate and enjoy!
